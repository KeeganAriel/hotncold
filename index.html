How Does This Work?

This is a guessing game, and you play like this:

  • 1. The computer will generate a random secret number between 1 to 100 and keep it hidden.
  • 2. You will get feedback on how close ("hot") or far ("cold") your guess is.
  • 3. You should keep guessing numbers until you can find the hidden one!
